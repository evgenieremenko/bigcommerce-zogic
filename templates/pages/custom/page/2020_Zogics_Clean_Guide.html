Why Choose Concentrates?



Commercial cleaners are sold as either ready-to-use products or in concentrated form for dilution. Using concentrated cleaners has many benefits when it comes to sustainability and getting the most bang for your buck.



Huge Cost Savings

Concentrated bottles can yield up to 32 gallons of ready-to-use cleaner—as low as .25 cents per gallon.


Drastically Reduce Waste

Since you’re diluting concentrated liquids, the amount of plastic you will discard is much lower than using ready-to-useproduct. 1 concentrate. 1 spray bottle. 100s of uses.


Recyclable Bottles

Zogics bottles are 100% recyclable. The eco-friendly benefits go beyond our fomulas—each bottle can be recycled when emply.

How Concentrates Cut Cleaning Costs



Zogics concentrated cleaning products are super cost-effective and will stretch your dollar as far as it can go. Beyond saving on big freight expenses that are typical of ready-to-use cleaners (you’re essentially paying to ship water), each bottle of Zogics concentrated cleaner will give you several spray bottle fills or gallon buckets to work with.


NOTE: Zogics cleaning concentrates each have recommended dilution rates, but our products are designed to be flexible when it comes to dilution. Have a particularly grimy section of flooring? No problem—increase your dilution rate a smidge.

How to calculate cost savings

Divide the product cost by the dilution rate

For example, our General Purpose Cleaner has a recommended dilution rate of 1:32 (1 ounce of cleaner to 32 ounces of water). Dividing the product cost by the dilution will show you that each quart of General Purpose Cleaner costs you only 0.28 cents!
