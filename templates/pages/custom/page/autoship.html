FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

 
How do I setup an autoshipment?
1. On eligible product pages, select YES to subscribe for Auto-Ship.
2. Next, select your Frequency from the drop down selection.
3. Place your order, and your autoshipment setup is complete.

How do I manage my autoshipments?
1. Login to your account.
2. Select View Subscriptions.
3. Within this menu, you can edit your payment option, skip a delivery if needed, update your shipping address, view upcoming deliveries, or cancel your subscription.

How do I update my credit card information?
1. Login to your account.
2. Select View Subscriptions.
3. Click on Payments Options
4. Here you can Add a Credit or Debit Card as well as edit or delete current card information.

How do I skip a delivery?
1. Login to your account.
2. Select View Subscriptions.
3. Click on Skip your next delivery

How do I cancel my subscription?
1. Login to your account.
2. Select View Subscriptions.
3. Click on Cancel subscription.
